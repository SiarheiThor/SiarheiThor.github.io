Welcome to my GitHub page!

I'm Siarhei Thor, a dedicated Data Scientist with a passion for transforming the way we understand and utilize data. Around 2020, I have started to transition into the data analysis and data science realm and have been developing my skills ever since.

My journey started in parametric design and digitalization, optimizing performance within building design. This unique experience has given me a keen eye for detail and a deep appreciation for the intersection of technology and creativity. After a successful career in architecture, leading large projects and developing my own, I lost my passion for architecture and gradually moved away from it into the realm of data science and AI, mostly through self-study and personal projects.

In my free time, as former elite athlete in orienteering, I am a huge fan of sports, especially trail running, orienteering, and recently all types of cycling — from long trips through the Alps to racing down technical downhill on a mountain bike. I also enjoy taking nice photographs from time to time.

Feel free to explore my GitHub, even though most of my interesting projects are not public, there might be some interesting stuff there, otherwise let's connect and see if I can impress you with some of my private projects.
