Welcome to my GitHub page.

I'm Siarhei Thor, an AI/ML engineer focused on applied machine learning, LLM systems, and the backend infrastructure around AI products. Around 2020, I started moving from architecture and computational design into data science and AI, and I have been building that path through work, study, and personal projects ever since.

My earlier work in parametric design and digitalization gave me a practical feel for messy real-world systems, automation, and the point where technology meets creative judgment. After a long architecture chapter, I gradually moved toward data science and AI, mostly through self-study, product experiments, and engineering work.

Outside work, as a former elite athlete in orienteering, I am a huge fan of sports, especially trail running, orienteering, and cycling, from long trips through the Alps to technical mountain bike trails. I also enjoy photography from time to time.

Feel free to explore my GitHub. Most of my more interesting projects are private, but there may still be something useful there. Otherwise, let's connect.
